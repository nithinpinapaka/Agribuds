About us

AGRIBUDS is a team where every individual aim is to help farmers without expecting anything in return but only to satisfy their needs and fulfil their dreams to certain extent.
  • Our aim is to help farmers without expecting any money in return and to fulfill their needs to certain extent.
  • We are here to fill the gap between a farmer and the government.
  • Our survey proved that water scarcity is a biggest problem faced by many farmers in different villages.
  • We will take in accordance each and every problem that are headed to us and thus strive to eradicate them.
  • This may not be a financial support but to help them by heading a problem to the government.

  • Many helping hands together would lead to success.
